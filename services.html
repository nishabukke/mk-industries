What We Do

Empowering Your Business with Cost-Effective Solutions

At M & K Industries, we offer a comprehensive suite of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients in the engineering and manufacturing sectors.

Heavy Structural Fabrication

Our heavy structural fabrication facility offers the capability to manufacture large and complex fabricated components using state-of-the-art equipment and skilled personnel.

Fuel Handling Equipments

Our company specializes in the manufacturing of fabricated fuel handling equipment, specifically pusher grates, traveling grates, dumping grates, and fuel feeding chutes.

Special Purpose Fabrication Solutions

The Special Purpose Fabrication Equipment division has created a variety of custom-made products to meet specific needs.

Sugar Products

Sugar products are designed to meet the specific needs of our clients in the sugar manufacturing industry, helping to streamline the manufacturing process and improve efficiency.

Blasting Painting & Export Packing

A blasting and painting facility is a specialized facility that offers surface preparation services for various industries.

Erection & Installation

We provide professional erection and installation services, ensuring that all structures and equipment are assembled and installed with precision and safety according to project specifications.

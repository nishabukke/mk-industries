Special Purpose Fabrication Solutions

Special Purpose Fabrication Solutions

The Special Purpose Fabrication Equipment division has created a variety of custom-made products to meet specific needs. These products include Foundation Bolts, which are used to secure structures to a foundation, Packers Plates which are used to level machinery and equipment, and Pressure Part supports that provide support to the pressure parts of the boiler, such as the steam drum, water walls, super heaters, and economizers.

Fabricated Silencers which are used to reduce noise levels, Fuel Feeding Chutes with Refractory application that help transport fuel, Ball Type Hand Railings which provide support and safety in Industrial Boilers , Fabricated Expansion Joints that allow for movement and flexibility to Ducting , Fabricated Dampers with Actuators that help control airflow, Staircase Grating and Railing Assembly that provide safe access to elevated areas, Peep Hole Assembly which allows inspection through a small opening, and Oil Coolers that help regulate temperature.

These fabricated products are designed and manufactured to meet specific requirements and are made with high-quality materials to ensure durability and reliability.

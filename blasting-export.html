Blasting Painting & Export Packaging

Blasting Painting & Export Packaging Solutions

Overall, our company specializes in the manufacturing of high-quality special purpose sugar industry products, including juice distributors, falling film evaporators, flash recovery systems, juice heaters, scrubbers, storage silos, sugar dryers, mill base frames, and Donnelly chutes, using advanced fabrication techniques and quality control procedures. These products are designed to meet the specific needs of our clients in the sugar manufacturing industry, providing reliable and efficient performance to improve their manufacturing processes.

We provides comprehensive services for Surface Preparation, Painting, and Export Packing. Our offerings include:

Surface preparation services such as Grit blasting and Painting,

Painting services that cater to various requirements such as HB Zinc Phosphate, HB Zink Silicate, Alkyd Zinc Phosphate, Epoxy Zinc Phosphate, Zinc Silicate, Synthetic Enamel, Polyurethane, and Fire Protection Paints as per clients' specifications.

Our Special Services for the oil and gas industries include Railing with galvanizing, Blasting, and PU Painting on Galvanized Surface

Our testing facility offers various tests such as DFT, Adhesion, Cross Cut, Dolly Test, and Holiday Test to ensure the quality and durability of our services.

We also have an in-house facility for export packing and factory stuffing, customized as per our customers' specific needs.

Once the surface has been prepared, the facility also provides painting services. The painting process involves applying a protective coating to the cleaned surface using specialized equipment such as spray guns, rollers, or brushes. The coating may be a single or multi-layered, depending on the specific needs of the client.

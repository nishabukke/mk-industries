Pioneer Excellence

Pioneering Excellence in Industrial Fabrication Projects

At M & K Industries, we take pride in pioneering excellence in industrial fabrication projects. Our expertise encompasses a wide range of fabrication services, including structural steel fabrication, custom metalwork, and complex assembly operations.

Fabrication and Supply of Bolted Structure Assembly to Nigeria's Largest Refinery

Scope includes comprehensive services from planning to delivery at Mumbai Port.

Involves detailed design engineering, procurement, manufacturing, mock-up assembly, inspection, NDT, surface treatment, painting, export packing, and container stuffing.

Supply of Fabricated Columns and Girders to Leading Steel Industry in India

Contributed to the development of a steel plant with high-quality structural components.

Supply of Platform Assembly and Walkway Assembly to Mumbai Metro Project

Played a crucial role in urban infrastructure development by supplying essential assemblies.

Supply of 700 MT Ball Type Hand Railings to RIL and Dangote Refinery

Delivered specialized railings with garnite blasting and painting post-galvanization through prestigious client Thermax Limited.

Supply of Travelling Grates and Pusher Grate Assemblies

Executed domestic and export projects with comprehensive solutions, including in-house trial runs during project execution.

Supply of Various Fabricated Components to Domestic and Export Projects

Includes columns, beams, bracings, girders, staircase, railings, ducting, boiler casings, hoppers, bunkers, silos, and boiler support structures.

Development of New Heavy Fabrication Facility

Expanded with a 40,000 square meter facility, including 15,000 square meters covered under overhead cranes for handling heavy fabrication jobs.

Repeat Project Contracts from Esteemed Clients

Testament to M&K Industries' credibility and reliability over the past 15 years, reinforcing trust and partnership.

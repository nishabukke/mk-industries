M&K Industries serves a wide range of industries including power, oil & gas, boiler manufacturing, chemical, cement, construction, sugar, and manufacturing equipment sectors.

M&K Industries specializes in heavy structural fabrication, fabricated fuel handling equipment, industrial special purpose products for the boiler and process industry, and fabricated products for the sugar industry.

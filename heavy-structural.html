Heavy Structural Fabrication Solutions

Our heavy structural fabrication facility offers the capability to manufacture large and complex fabricated components using state-of-the-art equipment and skilled personnel. The facility ensures high- quality standards are met by utilizing advanced cutting, bending, welding, and finishing techniques, and rigorous quality control and testing procedures.

Columns, Beams, Bracings, Girders, Base Frames, Racks, Trusses, Bolted Structures.

Ducting (Round and Square), Insulated Ducting, Boiler Casing, Hopper, Bag Filter for ESP, Fan Casing.

Chimney, Piping, Chimney Supporting structure, Boiler Supporting Structure.

Economizer Casings, Wind Box Assembly, Bunker, Silo, Mechanical a Dust Collector, Bed Plate for boiler Assembly, Fabricated Dampers

Stainless Steel and Alloy Steel Casings, SS and MS Piping and Pipes Supports. Stainless Steel Fabrications.

Tanks and Chimney Fabrication.

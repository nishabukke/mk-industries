Fuel Handling Equipments

Fuel Handling Equipment Solutions

Our company specializes in the manufacturing of fabricated fuel handling equipment, specifically pusher grates, traveling grates, dumping grates, and fuel feeding chutes. These types of equipment are essential for handling fuel, such as wood chips, biomass, coal, and waste material, in power plants and other industrial settings.

Pusher grates are used to move fuel along a combustion chamber or furnace, with a series of pusher bars that push the fuel forward. Traveling grates use a continuous conveyor system to move the fuel along the combustion chamber, with each grate section moving in a cyclical pattern. Dumping grates are used to dump the fuel onto the combustion bed from a higher level. Fuel feeding chutes are used to transfer fuel from a storage bin to the combustion chamber.

Our fabrication process for these fuel handling equipment involves multiple steps. It begins with designing the equipment to meet the client's specifications, the fabrication process involves cutting, welding, and assembling the various components, such as the pusher bars, conveyor system, or dumping mechanisms, using advanced welding techniques and equipment.

Once the equipment is fabricated, it undergoes rigorous testing and quality control procedures to ensure that it meets the required standards for safety, durability, and efficiency. The testing process may include simulated operating conditions to verify the equipment's performance and identify any potential issues. Overall, our company specializes in the manufacturing of high-quality fuel handling equipment, including pusher grates, traveling grates, dumping grates, and fuel feeding chutes, using advanced fabrication techniques and quality control procedures. These types of equipment are critical components in power plants and other industrial settings that rely on the efficient handling and combustion of fuel to produce energy.

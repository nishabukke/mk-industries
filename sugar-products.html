Sugar Products

Sugar Product Solutions

Our company specializes in the manufacturing of special purpose fabricated sugar industry products, including juice distributors, falling film evaporators, flash recovery systems, juice heaters, scrubbers, storage silos, sugar dryers, mill base frames, and Donnelly chutes. These products are designed to meet the specific needs of our clients in the sugar manufacturing industry, helping to streamline the manufacturing process and improve efficiency.

Juice distributors are used to distribute juice evenly over the evaporator tubes in the falling film evaporator, where it is heated and evaporated to concentrate the sugar. The flash recovery system is used to recover additional sugar from the evaporator, while the juice heater is used to heat the juice before it is sent to the next stage of the manufacturing process. Scrubbers are used to remove impurities from the exhaust gases produced during the manufacturing process, while storage silos are used to store the sugar before it is packaged for distribution. Sugar dryers are used to dry the sugar before packaging, while mill base frames provide structural support for the heavy machinery used to crush the sugar cane and extract the juice. Donnelly chutes guide the extracted juice from the mill to the next stage in the manufacturing process.
